* Added these solvers under the "Models" section:

  * Gradient

  * ConjugateGradient

  * GaussNewton

  * LevenbergMarquardt
 
  * IterativeReweighted

  * SupportVectorRegressionGradientVariant

  * NegativeBinomialRegression
 
  * GammaRegression
 
  * BinaryRegression
 
  * SupportVectorMachineGradientVariant

  * LevenbergMarquardtRegression
