# Measurement Of Engagement

Note: Almost all of these involves some form of psychological bias called the "sunk cost fallacy".

| Measurement Of Engagement            | Relationship To Engagement                                                                                                                                                        | Example Games That Have High Level Of This Measurement |
|--------------------------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|--------------------------------------------------------|
| Session Length                       | The more the player is engaged, the longer the player stays.                                                                                                                      | StarCraft, StarCraft 2                                 |
| Map Coverage                         | The more the player is engaged, the more the player explores. Even if the player lost, confused, or unable to find the objective, it implies problem-solving which is engagement. | Genshin Impact, Arknights: Endfield                    |
| Variety Of Items Collected           | The more the player is engaged, the more the player collect different items.                                                                                                      | Arknights, Blue Archive                                |
| Amount Of Resources Spent            | The more the player is engaged, the more the player spends on resources.                                                                                                          | Genshin Impact, Arknights: Endfield                    |
| Quest Completion                     | The more the player is engaged, the more the player spends time on completing quests.                                                                                             | Genshin Impact, Arknights: Endfield                    |
| Effective Actions Per Minute         | The more the player is engaged, the more intentional the player performs their actions.                                                                                           | StarCraft, StarCraft 2                                 |
| Number Of Online Players In A Server | It is related To players' individual session length, where more players overlap means very high session length per player due to potential player-to-player interactions.         | Fortnite, Call Of Duty: Warzone                        |
